### Targeting
- **Audience:** Solo contractors, small crew owners (painters, roofers, general contractors, handymen)

## CREATIVE ROTATION STRATEGY

### Week 1-2: Problem awareness
- Run Ad 1 (Lost Job) and Ad 2 (Receipt Shoebox) to cold audiences
- Goal: Get engagement, build awareness of pain points

### Week 3-4: Solution introduction
- Run Ad 4 (60-Second Quote) to people who engaged with Ads 1-2
- Run Ad 3 (Office Manager) to cold audience + engagers
- Goal: Show the product, introduce value

### Week 5+: Retargeting + scaling
- Run Ad 5 (Bilingual) to Hispanic segments continuously
- Retarget all engagers with Ad 4 (product demo)
- Scale winning ads

### Seasonal overlays
- Jan-Apr: Boost Ad 2 (receipts/tax)
- May-Aug: Focus on Ad 1 (lost jobs, busy season)
- Sept-Dec: Ad 3 (office manager, planning for next year)
